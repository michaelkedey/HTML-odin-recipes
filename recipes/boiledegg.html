Boiled Egg

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken Egg
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Stew

Steps

  1. prepare your stew
  2. put a pot of water on fire
  3. add your egg when the water starts to boil
  4. let your egg cook for 3 minutes
  5. add some salt while the egg boils/li>
  6. take out your egg after 3 minutes
  7. peel of the shells and serve sliced or whole with your stew
